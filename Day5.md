## Key Learnings

### Popular but poorly rated products
High review count but low ratings observed in printers, cables, USB hubs.
Indicates necessity purchases despite poor user experience.

### Hidden gems
High ratings with low review count.
Indicates discovery gap and marketing opportunity.

### Discount vs rating
Heavy discounts do not guarantee higher ratings.
Some high-discount products show lower average ratings.

### Category trust
Electronics and accessories categories show higher average ratings than others.

## Insight

Data reveals customer behavior patterns:
- purchase driven by need vs satisfaction
- quality perception not tied to discount
- early-stage winners exist with low visibility
