# Day 4 — Pricing & Perception Analysis

## Objective
Understand relationship between price, discount, reviews and ratings.

## Findings

1. Higher price does not guarantee higher rating.
2. Extreme discounts do not automatically indicate poor quality.
3. Products with very high review volumes stabilize between 4.1–4.4 rating.

## Key Concept Learned
- Data cleaning for currency symbols
- Statistical convergence (regression to mean)
- Marketplace pricing psychology

## Reflection
Volume matters more than extremes.
Market behaviour is statistical, not emotional.
